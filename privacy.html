Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 7, 2026

Word Pilot ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights when using the Word Pilot mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign in using Google Sign-In or Firebase Authentication, we may collect your name, email address, and profile picture (if available) to create and manage your account.
  • User Data: Vocabulary progress, saved words, quiz results, preferences, and other learning data you choose to store are securely saved in Cloud Firestore so they can be synchronized across your devices.
  • Usage Analytics: We use Google Analytics for Firebase to collect anonymous usage information such as app screens visited, session duration, device model, operating system version, and app performance.
  • Crash Reports: Firebase Crashlytics automatically collects crash reports and diagnostic information to help improve app stability.
  • Push Notifications: Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) generates a unique device token so we can send important notifications and learning reminders.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the app.
  • Synchronize your learning progress across devices.
  • Improve features and user experience.
  • Monitor app performance and fix technical issues.
  • Send optional notifications about learning and app updates.

3. Third-Party Services

Word Pilot uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)

These services may collect and process information according to their own privacy policies.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Your information is shared only with the service providers listed above to operate, maintain, and improve the app.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your information while your account remains active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at monir.xcd@gmail.com.

After verification, we will delete your data within a reasonable period unless we are legally required to retain certain information.

6. Data Security

We use Firebase's secure infrastructure and industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

Word Pilot is intended for students and learners. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without appropriate parental consent. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

8. International Data Transfers

Because Word Pilot uses Google Firebase services, your information may be processed or stored on servers located in countries outside your country of residence.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the updated Effective Date will indicate when the changes become effective.
